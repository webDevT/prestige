Aqua Jazz Lunch

King Street Wharf – Darling Harbour
BOOK Online

What better way to enjoy lunch time than on Sydney Harbour?

Come on board and sway to the rhythms of live Jazz performances and indulge in a delicious Seafood and Carvery Lunch accompanied by a selection of fresh fruit, tea and coffee. Enjoy a glass of bubbly from the bar and let time pass you by as you embrace the spectacular harbour views!

Talented Jazz musicians with melodious Jazz and Blues tunes will unwind you, whilst the aroma of a delicious seafood and carvery buffet freshly prepared on board will whet your appetite.
